Video game franchises

What's the deal with Video Games?

As of now, video games have become very popular for many people to play, with a wide variety and selection to choose from. There are almost an endless amount of video games out there, some more prominient than others, and having another instalment that follows the first one, then having a steady flow of installments coming out. Those are mostly known as video game franchises.

Popular Video Game Franchises

You might already be aware of some popular video game franchises, having either heard of them, or playing some of their instalments. One that's well known by almost everyone would be Super Mario Bros, since the character Mario himself, is already an iconic character, and has games outside of the main instalments that are also well known and popular, such as Mario Party, and Mario Kart. There are other examples, such as Sonic the Hedgehog, Kirby, and many more.
